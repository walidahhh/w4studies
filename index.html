About Me

Education Enthusiast

Hello! I’m Walidah, the founder of W4Studies, an enthusiastic private tutor, and an incoming student at Queen Mary University of London (QMUL), where I will be studying Mathematics and Computer Science. My journey in education is fuelled by a deep-seated passion for helping students unlock their full potential and achieve academic success.

The idea behind W4Studies originated from my own educational experiences and a desire to make a meaningful impact on students' lives. Having faced various academic challenges myself, I realised the importance of personalised support. I wanted to establish a platform where students could receive bespoke guidance, not only to excel in their studies but also to build confidence and a genuine love for learning.

Throughout my academic career, I have achieved impressive results, including 3 A’s in A-Level Mathematics, Biology, and Psychology, as well as a Grade 8 in GCSE Mathematics and English Language. These achievements reflect my commitment and understanding of the academic hurdles students encounter. My forthcoming studies at QMUL in Mathematics and Computer Science will further enhance my expertise, allowing me to offer even more insightful and effective tutoring.

At W4Studies, our approach is centred around the belief that every student has unique strengths and potential. My teaching philosophy focuses on creating engaging, interactive, and customised tutoring sessions that cater to individual learning styles. My goal is to make learning not only effective but also enjoyable, fostering an environment where students feel motivated and supported.

W4Studies aims to inspire and empower students to reach their highest potential. I am dedicated to providing a nurturing and dynamic learning experience that encourages curiosity, critical thinking, and a lifelong passion for learning. By sharing my experiences and knowledge, I hope to motivate students to set ambitious goals and pursue them with confidence.
